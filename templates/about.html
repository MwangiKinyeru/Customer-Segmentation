Standard Customer Segments

These segments are identified through RFM analysis and K-Means clustering of regular customer behavior patterns.

Cluster 3

Premium Customers

Behavior

Strongest customers who spend the most, buy most often, and purchased recently. Clear ongoing engagement driving large share of value.

Recommended Action

Offer early access, loyalty rewards, or personalized bundles. Encourage repeat spend with targeted cross-sell offers.

Cluster 0

Regular Customers

Behavior

Steady behavior with middle-range spending and frequency. Remain active and reliable, though not top spenders.

Recommended Action

Grow value by sending small incentives, product reminders, or curated recommendations. Nudge toward higher frequency with limited-time deals.

Cluster 2

Occasional Customers

Behavior

Infrequent buyers who spend less and purchase occasionally. Engage from time to time but not strong contributors.

Recommended Action

Re-engage using simple reminders, light discounts, or seasonal campaigns. Show products based on past purchases to spark another visit.

Cluster 1

Lapsed Customers

Behavior

Least active with low spending and frequency. Very high recency indicates long period since last purchase. At risk of churn or already inactive.

Recommended Action

Win-back plan with "we miss you" messages and strong offers. If no response, move to low-cost marketing list to avoid unnecessary spend.

Special Customer Segments

These customers exhibit exceptional behavior that falls outside normal patterns and require specialized attention.

Cluster A

High Spender Customers

Behavior

Extremely high monetary value but low frequency. Spend large amounts in few purchases. Recency varies widely.

Recommended Action

Encourage more frequent visits with personalized invitations, exclusive previews, or new stock reminders with small incentives.

Cluster B

Power Shopper Customers

Behavior

High frequency but moderate monetary value. Repeat buyers who come often but don't spend large amounts per visit. Moderate recency.

Recommended Action

Introduce loyalty programs, volume discounts, or bundles. Show higher-end items based on browsing/purchase history to grow value.

Cluster C

Elite Customers

Behavior

VIP customers who exhibit exceptional purchasing behavior with both high transaction values and frequent purchases. This top-tier segment represents our most valuable and engaged customers.

Recommended Action

Provide priority service, early access, and personalized communication. Avoid over-contacting but give meaningful rewards to maintain loyalty.
